[Plugin Name] Example Editor Content Assist Plugin

[Describe what your plugin does] The orion.edit.contentAssist service contributes content assist providers to the editor. A content assist provider produces suggestions for text that may be inserted into the editor at a given point. Providers are invoked when the user triggers the "content assist" action by pressing Ctrl+Space in the editor. More advanced content assist providers will generally use the buffer text or the AST provided in the editorContext object.
The example below will provide completion for tags of tags named after one of the 8 planets in our solar system. After completion the cursor will be positioned between the newly created tags.
